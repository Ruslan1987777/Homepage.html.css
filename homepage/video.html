Ruslan Baiazitov incorporates a synesthetic approach in his projects, interpreting the Études through a narrative trajectory that connects sound with imagery, color, and emotional flow. By treating each work as part of a larger unfolding story, he transforms virtuosic repertoire into immersive artistic experiences. His projects, supported by Steinway Piano Gallery Houston, have received recognition for their originality and innovative fusion of music and visual imagination.
